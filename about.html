The Problem We Solve

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2018

Founded in New Delhi

Phenix Biogas incorporated with a mandate to deploy thermophilic anaerobic digestion at commercial scale across India's urban waste corridors.

2020

First Pilot Plant Commissioned

5 TPD pilot facility in Pune achieved 94% volatile solids destruction, validating our thermophilic process design under Indian climatic conditions.

2022

MNRE Approval & SATAT Onboarding

Recognized as an approved Bio-CNG developer under the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy and onboarded as a SATAT scheme partner.

2024

Gujarat Mega-Plant Operational

Commissioned India's largest private-sector thermophilic biogas plant at 50 TPD capacity in Gujarat, processing agricultural residues into grid-ready Bio-CNG.
