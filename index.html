The Phenix Process

From Waste to Wealth

Our thermophilic anaerobic digestion systems convert mixed organic waste — municipal solid waste, agricultural residues, and industrial effluents — into Bio-CNG, renewable electricity, and bio-fertilizer.

01 / Feedstock Intake

Municipal solid waste, agro-residues, and industrial effluents are received, sorted, and pre-processed through our automated triage systems to optimize digester loading rates.

02 / Thermophilic Digestion

Proprietary thermophilic bioreactors operating at 50–55°C accelerate anaerobic breakdown up to 3x faster than conventional systems, achieving over 88% volatile solids destruction.

03 / Clean Energy Output

Raw biogas is upgraded via membrane separation to 98%+ pure Bio-CNG for grid injection or vehicle fuel. Digestate is pelletized into certified organic bio-fertilizer.

Core Technology

Thermophilic Smart Digester

Our systems maintain continuous thermophilic conditions within a tight 50–55°C band, which hyper-accelerates metabolic degradation kinetics compared to conventional mesophilic operations running at 35–37°C.

Real-time monitoring of volatile fatty acid (VFA) to alkalinity ratios provides predictive protection against organic overloading — a common failure mode in competing systems.
