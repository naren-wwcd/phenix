Phase 1: Hydrolysis

Phase 2: Acidogenesis

Phase 3: Acetogenesis

VFAs are further oxidized by acetogenic bacteria into acetate, hydrogen, and carbon dioxide — the direct precursors for methane generation.

Strict methanogenic archaebacteria rapidly fractionate inputs into targeted clean utility assets with standard methane purity levels above 98%.
